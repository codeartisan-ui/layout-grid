Margins and Gutters

Material design grid layout may have different margin and gutter width, rather than fixed one. It follows an 8dp square baseline grid and can adapt 8, 16, 24 or 40px wide. But in our framework, we are using 8, 16 and 24 only. 40px margin or gutter are rarely used and can be excluded for now.

Default is 16px gutter and margin
